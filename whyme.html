Why Me?

Hi again, I'm really looking forward to joining the Founders & Coders programme. Here is a little more about me.

My interest in programming started in university, but I began actively working towards it a few months ago when I decided to build a custom website & for my photographs. You can check it out here.


I started learning HTML5 and CSS3 using the online resources Free Code Camp, Scrimba & Frontend Mentor. I am particularly interested in front-end development but keen to learn a variety of tech stacks. To date, I have completed the first 300 hours of Free Code Camp's responsive web design, basic Javascript, ES6, Basic Data Structures, and basic algorithm scripting. I'm still working on the rest of the Javascript module, and I will complete it in the next few weeks. I have also joined Codebar and attended several coding meet-ups.


I want to join the Founders and Coders to take me from a hobbyist website maker to a professional programmer. After three months of self-studying, the Founders and Coders programme is the next step in helping me join the world of tech and reach my goal of working in the industry.


I believe I am right for the Founders and Coders programme because the hundreds of hours of consistent effort to learn to code independently show my dedication to achieving my goal of becoming a full-time developer.
