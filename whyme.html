Why Me?

Hello everyone, I'm looking forward to joining the Founders & Coders programme. Here is a little more about me.

My interest in programming started in university, but I began actively working towards it a few months ago. I enjoy photography in my spare time, and I decided to build a custom website for my photographs. If you have time, you can check it out here.


I started learning HTML5 and CSS3 using the online resources freeCodeCamp, Scrimba & Frontend Mentor. I am particularly interested in front-end development but keen to learn a variety of tech stacks. To date, I have completed more than 300 hours of freeCodeCamp's Responsive Web Design, Basic Javascript, ES6, Basic Data Structures, and Basic Algorithm Scripting. I'm still working on the rest of the Javascript modules, but I am currently focusing on the Founders and Coders application process. As well as hundreds of hours of self-study, I also attend weekly coding meet-ups at Codebar, a charity that enables minority groups to learn coding in a safe space.


I recently met a coder who has now become a friend. They introduced me to the Founders and Coders programme. I want to join this course to take me from a hobbyist website maker to a professional programmer. I was drawn to Founders and Coders because you want to make the tech industry more diverse and inclusive for everyone. The opportunity to learn in a safe environment will help everyone reach their goals. After several months of self-studying, this programme is the next step in helping me join the world of tech and reach my goal of working in the industry.


I believe I am right for the Founders and Coders programme because the hundreds of hours of consistent effort to learn to code independently show my dedication to achieving my goal of becoming a full-time developer. Thanks for your time. Click here to see my projects page and find out what I have done throughout the application process.
